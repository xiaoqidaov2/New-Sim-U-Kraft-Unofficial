# English Version

**New: Sim-U-Kraft (NSUK) - The Spiritual Sequel to the Classic Sim-U Metropolis**

Ever dreamed of being the mayor of your own Minecraft world? **New: Sim-U-Kraft (NSUK)** brings the ultimate city-building experience to modern versions.

This is not just an update—**NSUK is a complete rewrite from the ground up, built as a spiritual sequel** to the Sim-U-Kraft mod. While we deeply respect the original classic, we haven't simply copy-pasted its code. Instead, we've distilled its soul: the joy of hiring NPCs, managing coins, and watching a bustling town rise from the dirt. On this foundation, we've introduced deep optimizations, refined gameplay mechanics, and innovative features that bring the city sim experience into the modern Minecraft era—smoother, smarter, and more ambitious than ever before.

**What kind of mayor will you be?**
Start with just 20 gold coins. Hire specialized NPC workers—from Bakers and Butchers to Farmers and Builders—to form the backbone of your economy. Plan residential, commercial, and industrial zones, manage your town budget, and gradually transform a barren plot of land into a thriving metropolis.

**Core Features:**
- **Complete Rewrite & Modernization** - Built for **Minecraft 1.20.1 Forge** with zero legacy code, ensuring stability and compatibility with the modern Minecraft engine
- **NPC Economy System** - Employ lots of interactive NPCs across diverse jobs to build a functional, self-sustaining city economy
- **Flexible Building System** - Place structures, photocopy existing builds, and design your skyline just the way you envision it
- **Resource & Tax Management** - Balance income and expenditure, collect taxes, and manage resources to fund your city's expansion
- **Building Pack Management (via NSUK: Simupack)** - Enhance your city with community-made building packs by installing them just like normal mods, using the companion addon **NSUK: Simupack**
- **Optimized Multiplayer** - Designed with smoother multiplayer performance in mind—collaborate or compete with friends to build the greatest city

If you remember the charm of the old days but crave the polish of the new, this is the golden age of Minecraft city-building.

**Getting Started:**
1. Install Forge for Minecraft 1.20.1
2. Drop the NSUK `.jar` into your `/mods` directory
3. Launch the game, start a new world, **craft a City Core, place it down, and right-click it** to found your first city and begin your mayoral journey!

# 中文

**新：模拟大都市（NSUK）—— 经典 Sim-U 都市模组的“精神续作”**

想在 Minecraft 里真正过一把当市长的瘾吗？《新：模拟大都市》就是这样一款专注于城市经营的模组。

我们把它称作传奇模组 Sim-U-Kraft 的**“精神续作”**，原因很简单：它继承了旧作最让人上头的核心乐趣——雇佣形形色色的 NPC 村民、精打细算建设城镇、亲眼看着一片荒地变成繁华都市。但与此同时，**我们并不是死板地重置老版本，而是从零开始完全重写，并在老版本玩法的基础上做了大量优化和创新**。更干净的架构、更聪明的 NPC 行为、更流畅的联机体验，这就是 NSUK 想带给大家的“现代都市模拟”。

**你的市长之路从何开始？**
你的启动资金只有 **20 元**。你需要活用这笔钱，雇佣面包师、屠夫、农民、伐木工、建筑工等各色职业 NPC，让他们成为城市的第一批居民与经济支柱。规划住宅区、商业区、工业区，像真正的管理者一样调整税收、平衡财政，一步一步将脚下的不毛之地开拓成独一无二的大都会。

**核心亮点：**
- **彻底重写，拥抱新版本** - 专为 **Minecraft 1.20.1 Forge** 打造，基于全新的代码架构开发，彻底告别旧时代的崩溃与不兼容
- **真实的 NPC 经济** - 多种交互 NPC，每个居民都会参与生产、消费与工作，自发形成一套动态而真实的城市供需体系
- **自由的建造与规划** - 自由摆放建筑、随心所欲布置你的天际线
- **财政与资源管理** - 照看城市金库，权衡税收与支出，用良好的财政循环支撑城市扩张
- **建筑包管理（需搭配《模拟大都市：筑艺坊》）** - 通过安装附属模组 **《模拟大都市：筑艺坊》（NSUK: Simupack）**，像加载普通模组一样轻松管理社区建筑包，告别手动覆盖的繁琐
- **多人联机深度优化** - 相较旧版进行优化，再也不用担心 NPC 一多就卡顿，与好友合作或竞争，共建世界级都市

如果你怀念当年的感觉，又无法忍受旧模组的粗糙，那么 NSUK 就是为你准备的！

**如何开始？**
1. 为 Minecraft 1.20.1 安装好对应的 Forge
2. 将模组本体 `.jar` 文件放进 `mods` 文件夹
3. 进入游戏，新建世界，**合成一个“城市核心”，把它放在地上并右键点击**，你的第一座城市便会从此而起，宏大的开拓之旅就此展开！
